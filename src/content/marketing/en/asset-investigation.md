Before filing a lawsuit or enforcing a court judgment, it pays to know what assets the other party actually holds. **Detective Pulse** provides professional asset investigations — tracing and verifying a debtor's property so you can make informed legal and financial decisions.

## What we can trace

- Land and property holdings
- Registered vehicles
- Business interests and company involvement
- Other recoverable assets relevant to your claim

## Why it matters

Pursuing a debtor without knowing their assets often wastes time and legal cost. A clear asset picture tells you whether enforcement is worthwhile and where to focus. We work systematically and report our findings in a clear, usable format.

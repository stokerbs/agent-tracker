## What are detective services?

Detective services provide advice and investigation on matters tied to the doubts or worries that come up in daily life. Private investigators have the skills and tools to find information that resolves a client's questions effectively — personal, business or safety-related. Put simply, detective services help you find answers you can't find yourself, usually by gathering information, conducting surveillance, or searching for clues that lead to clarity.

## What do detective services include?

- **[Infidelity investigators](/en/cheating-spouse-investigator)** — checking for and gathering evidence of an unfaithful partner. This is delicate work that demands great care and the strictest confidentiality.
- **[Cyber / IT investigators](/en/cyber-investigation)** — specialists who examine and uncover information hidden online: data fraud, verifying the truth of social-media claims, even recovering deleted information.
- **Asset investigators** — if you wonder where an asset is, or suspect an owner is concealing details about land or property, an asset investigator finds information about the asset, ownership history, permits, and other legal details that may affect your investment.
- **Vehicle tracing** — a missing or stolen car is a problem many face; a specialist uses skill and experience to follow the trail.
- **Gathering evidence for child-custody claims** — a detective can help you gather relevant evidence, such as checking the other party's behaviour, to prove who can better care for the child.
